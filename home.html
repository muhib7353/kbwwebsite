    About the KABIRIANS


    Khaild Bin Waleed High School was established in 15 october,1993 initially with the investment of only Rs.1000.The school faces many ups and downs from 1193 to 2008.IN 2006 the school construction was started and successfully the school was made affiliated with BISE,Multan in 2006.And now in 2022, school has shifted in its own airy,vast,splendid and second to none double storey building in kabirwal with beautiful grassy plots ,assembly halll,ply ground,stand by electric power system,electric water coolers,cycle stand ,library,computer and scienece labs,canteens and hostel.Whole of its building is security protected.
